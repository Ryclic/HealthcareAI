What do we do?

We provide rough AI diagnosis for emerging third world countries lacking medical personnel capacity.
As of now, we are working towards providing an accurate AI model for detecting heart failure and other heart conditions based off of known data markers.
Users can submit their avaliable health markers and receive a sample diagnosis with up to ~90% accuracy.
